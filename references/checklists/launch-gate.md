# Launch Gate Checklist

## Launch Gate Checklist

- Locale resolution has been tested for anonymous and authenticated users.
- RTL and pseudolocale checks have been run where applicable.
- Time-zone-sensitive flows have been tested with representative regions.
- Capability matrices are current for providers, payment methods, and markets in scope.
- Billing and invoice outputs have been reviewed for the target seller role and market.
- Privacy notices, consent paths, and rights workflows are aligned with the actual processing behavior.
- Telemetry dashboards and alerts exist for the new locale, market, or region.
- Rollout and rollback steps are defined.
- A human reviewer has accepted any legal, compliance, or residency assumptions that could not be proven in code alone.
