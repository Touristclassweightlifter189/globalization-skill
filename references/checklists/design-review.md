# Design Review Checklist

## Design Review Checklist

- Is the task classified correctly by layer and policy type?
- Which cross-cutting dimensions are in scope?
- What is canonical and what is localized?
- What is the precedence order for locale, time zone, currency, and market?
- Which parts are standards, official guidance, engineering inference, or team policy?
- What are the tenant and security boundaries?
- What are the privacy, consent, residency, or rights implications?
- What are the billing or seller-role implications?
- Which adapters and overlays are needed?
- How will success and failure be observed after launch?
